FAQ

Is my information private?
We only use the information you submit to serve and improve the product, we will not sell or distribute your data.
Do you train your LLM on my data?
The models we use do not train on submitted data. You can read more about these APIs here.
Why is Castora better than ChatGPT or Gemini for Systems Engineers?
Castora has been created to be consistently reliable when generating and evaluating requirements, even when the context window & amount of information gets extremely large. It is less likely to omit certain details, reducing your risk of creating logically inconsistent requirements due to “forgetting” details, because it leverages multiple models and coordinates between them. Try it out and compare - we think you will like what you see!
Why shouldn’t I just create my own version of this?
You could create a GPT or Gem, but then you’d have to build it. We already did the hard work! Training it on systems engineering logic, standards, and verification methods, building custom workflows to ingest/export DOORS, Jama, etc., and spend time validating its reliability and accuracy. Speed up your workflows today, with Castora!
