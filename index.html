FAQ

Is Castora only for Systems Engineers?
Castora is designed for all engineers who develop, review, manage, and implement Systems Requirements - not just Systems Engineers!
Is my information private?
Castora implements a security-first approach to data management and processing. User information, such as design descriptions, documentation, and feedback, are encrypted with user credentials. Passwords are hashed and salted before storing - meaning that only users can access their own data.
Do you train your LLM on my data?
The models we use do not train on submitted data.
Why is Castora better than ChatGPT or Gemini for Systems Engineers?
Castora is designed to be consistently reliable when generating and evaluating requirements, even when the context window & amount of information gets extremely large. It is less likely to omit certain details, reducing your risk of creating logically inconsistent requirements due to “forgetting” details, because it leverages multiple models and coordinates between them.

Many systems engineers are already working with LLMs to develop their requirements. This has several issues: it requires a manual feedback loop of copy-pasting, the LLM is missing detailed context about the broader system, LLMs overwriting requirements which were previously accepted, hallucinations and redundancies, unstructured responses, just to name a few. We eliminate the friction between your preferred LLMs and Requirements Management Tools.

We are excited to enable high quality engineering practices for every design, while simulataneously accelerating development processes.

Try it out and compare - we think you will like what you see!
Why shouldn’t I just create my own version of this?
You could theoretically create a set of GPTs or Gems which orchestrate multiple workflows and data, but existing LLM frameworks do not easily allow customization and integration between GPTs. We already did the hard work! Training it on systems engineering logic, standards, and verification methods, building custom workflows to ingest/export DOORS, Jama, etc., validating its reliability and accuracy.
